About

Hey, I’m Kelvin —a data analyst with a curious mind and a love for cracking tough problems. I like to think of myself as a detective for data, always looking for clues and patterns hidden in the numbers. My journey into data started with a simple question: why do people make the choices they do? Now, I get to dig into the ‘why’ behind those choices every day, helping companies make smarter, data-driven decisions. I’m passionate about using data to create stories that matter—stories that can influence decisions and create positive change. My favorite projects are the ones where I get to find creative solutions, whether that’s optimizing marketing strategies or predicting trends before they happen. There’s something really satisfying about finding that one insight that changes everything.

Outside of work, I’m all about exploring and staying curious. I enjoy planning weekend trips to discover new places, whether it’s a hidden hiking trail or a quirky local café. I’m also a bit of a documentary enthusiast—I can spend hours watching anything from historical deep dives to the latest on emerging tech trends. Fun fact: I’m learning to play the piano, mostly because I believe picking up new skills keeps life interesting and helps me bring a fresh perspective to my work
